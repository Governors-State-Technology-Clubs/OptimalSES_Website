About Us

Who We Are

Optimal SES is a family-owned Latino construction company providing comprehensive construction solutions for residential and commercial projects throughout the Chicago area. We're proud to serve our community with the same dedication, attention to detail, and respect that defines Latino family values.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build the future by partnering with clients to bring their vision to life. We focus on clear communication, thoughtful planning, and consistent execution. Whether you speak English or Spanish, we're here to listen and deliver results that exceed expectations.

Our Values

We value integrity, quality, and professionalism. We believe in long-term relationships with clients, subcontractors, and our team. As a family business, we treat every project like it's our own home and every client like family.

Licensed & Insured

Your peace of mind is our priority. Optimal SES is fully licensed and insured, meeting all state and local requirements. We stand behind our work and protect your investment with comprehensive coverage and professional accountability.
